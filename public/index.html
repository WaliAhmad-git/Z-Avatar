Quick Start
How It Works

1. Set your API keys in Settings (one time only — saved forever).

2. Schedule your classes with Google Meet links and time slots.

3. Virtual Avatar automatically joins Meet, listens for your name, responds via ElevenLabs voice, and takes exhaustive AI notes every 15 minutes.

4. Notes screen-records any video and generates comprehensive lecture notes.

Student Profile
Trigger Words
These are the words/phonetic variants of your name that the AI listens for. When Deepgram transcribes any of these, the avatar responds. Add all phonetic spellings teachers might use.
Examples: wali, willie, wally, hotak, volley, واللی
ElevenLabs Voice
If you provide an ElevenLabs API key, your avatar will speak with a realistic human voice instead of the default Microsoft edge-tts voice. The Gemini-generated answer text is sent to ElevenLabs and played through the virtual microphone.
Find voice IDs at elevenlabs.io/voice-lab. Leave blank for default.
